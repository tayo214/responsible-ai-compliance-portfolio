# AI Governance Stakeholder Matrix: Aura-Gen Project

This document identifies the key departments involved in the oversight of the Aura-Gen Customer Assistant and their specific areas of responsibility.

| Department | Role | Key Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Finance** | Risk & Budget Owner | Defines refund limits; Monitors for financial fraud and bot-driven revenue leakage. |
| **Legal & Compliance** | Regulatory Lead | Ensures alignment with the EU AI Act; Manages liability for automated decisions. |
| **IT & Security** | Technical Custodian | Manages self-hosted model security; Oversees data privacy and infrastructure. |
| **Customer Experience** | Business Owner | Owns the end-user impact; Monitors bot accuracy and customer satisfaction scores. |
| **Data Privacy Office** | Privacy Guardian | Conducts Privacy Impact Assessments (PIAs); Ensures compliance with GDPR/CCPA for internal data. |
