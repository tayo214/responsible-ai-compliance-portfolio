# AI Governance 30-60-90 Day Action Plan

This plan outlines the strategic approach for establishing an AI Governance framework, focusing on risk mitigation and stakeholder engagement.

| Phase | Goal | Key Objectives |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Days 1–30** | Discovery & Foundation | Map stakeholders; Finalize AI Systems Inventory; Establish Governance Committee. |
| **Days 31–60** | Risk & Policy | Draft Master AI Policy; Perform EU AI Act classification; Define escalation thresholds. |
| **Days 61–90** | Operationalization | Implement bias testing; Develop Incident Playbooks; Create Executive KPI Dashboard. |
